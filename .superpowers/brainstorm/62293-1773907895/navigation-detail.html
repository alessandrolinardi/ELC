Key decisions in this nav:

  • Tab-style switching — active tool gets gold underline, instant recognition
  • "Altro" overflow dropdown — when you add tools 4-5-6+, they go into a grouped dropdown menu (categorized by function)
  • Dev Mode toggle — top-right, unobtrusive. Turns on: log viewer, debug expanders, raw text panels
  • No sidebar at all — Streamlit sidebar is completely hidden, full width for content

Layout principles:

  • Centered content — max-width ~780px, easy to scan, no wall-of-text feel
  • Step indicator — always visible at top of each tool, highlights current step
  • Clean upload cards — large drop zones with clear labels and constraints
  • Help as a link, not an expander — "Come esportare da ShippyPro?" opens inline or as a modal, not a cluttered expander block
  • Warm palette — gold (#c4a265) as accent, warm whites (#fafaf8), subtle shadows
